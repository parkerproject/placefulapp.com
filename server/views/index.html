How Does Placeful Work?

Placeful provides you with a curated list of specials that are running at your local places (bars, restaurants, event venues, spas, florists, etc) at any given time. Anything noteworthy on the sidewalk chalkboards throughout the city goes into Placeful. Local spots update promos in real-time throughout the day, so you're always up to date. Take advantage of specials happening right now, or save specials that catch your eye for future plans.
